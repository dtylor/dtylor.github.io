Tylor Data Services, LLC (TDS) is a Small Business Administration (SBA) 8(a) certified and Women Owned Small Business (WOSB) founded in 2010.

TDS continues to deliver data science consulting, data analytics, and custom computer programming services that are proven to address business challenges for government agencies, non-profit organizations and private corporations. References from a range of clients, including the IRS, FINRA and prime contractor consulting firms, can be made available upon request.

TDS provides advanced analytics and big data solutions (using statistical, machine learning, and big data technologies), apply rapid development for BI solutions, and place a foundational focus on applied algorithms and advanced analytics (recent emphasis on graph analytics and graph-based AI).

The company has developed multiple solutions to support advanced analytics and address complex business problems. We look forward to collaborating with our clients to address their next challenge in deriving knowledge from data.
