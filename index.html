Tylor Data Services, LLC (TDS) is a Small Business Administration (SBA) 8(a) certified, Women Owned Small Business (WOSB) and Economically Disadvantaged Women Owned Small Business (EDWOSB) founded in 2010.

TDS continues to deliver data science consulting, big data analytics, and custom computer programming services that are proven to address business challenges for government agencies, non-profit organizations and private corporations. References from a wide range of pleased clients, including the IRS, FINRA and prime contractor consulting firms, can be made available upon request.

TDS provides advanced analytics and big data solutions (using intelligent statistical, machine learning, and big data technologies), apply rapid development for BI solutions, and place a foundational focus on applied algorithms and advanced analytics (parallel processing methods in Spark, use of Scala, graph frame, and machine learning featurization, classification, deep learning and graph clustering algorithms).

The company has developed multiple solutions to support advanced analytics and address complex business problems.We look forward to collaborating with our clients to tackle the next challenge in the ever-changing big data marketplace.
